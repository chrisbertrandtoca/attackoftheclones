### 📝 Executive Summary
In this installment of the space opera, we learn that the Jedi Order—the galaxy's premier peacekeepers—are remarkably easy to trick if you just delete a folder on their computer. Also, Yoda gets a lightsaber and suddenly thinks he's a pinball.

### 🚩 Key Plot Points (In Order of Absurdity)

1.  **The Library Problem:** Obi-Wan discovers that if a planet isn't in the Google Maps of the Jedi Archives, it simply does not exist. *Logic 100.*
2.  **The Diner Scene:** Obi-Wan visits a four-armed chef named Dexter Jettster to solve a mystery, because 800-year-old Jedi Masters don't know as much as a guy running a space-Waffle House.
3.  **The Rom-Com:** Anakin and Padmé go to Naboo to frolic in fields. Anakin explains his complex political philosophy (Dictatorship is fine, actually) and his deep-seated hatred for geological sediments.
4.  **The Factory Tour:** R2-D2 and C-3PO accidentally wander into a Droid Factory, turning a high-stakes rescue mission into a 10-minute platforming level from a video game.
